ChatNou will be an AI-powered, multilingual, WhatsApp-connected knowledge-sharing platform. It is designed to help trusted knowledge purveyors, such as researchers, NGOs, conservationists, and Indigenous leaders, transform their documents into simple, layperson-friendly Q&A insights, and deliver them directly to communities who need them most.

This MVP demonstrates how users can upload PDFs, automatically generate Q&A pairs, and interact with the resulting chatbot. Future versions will support Indigenous voice-note integration, enabling local community members to contribute their own Traditional Ecological Knowledge (TEK) to enrich the system.
